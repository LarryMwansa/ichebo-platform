Sceptre Community Programme

Church returned
to the community.

The Sceptre Community is the smallest legitimate expression of the church — a Church Node. As few as three or four believers. A family, a household fellowship, a neighbourhood gathering. What matters is not size but the legitimacy of function.

Every Sceptre Community carries the Collective Person principle: a constituted gathering of believers becomes an entity with its own gifts, calling, and responsibility before God. This is not metaphor. It is governance.
