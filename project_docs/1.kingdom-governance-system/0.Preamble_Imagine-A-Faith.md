
## **Imagine A Faith**  

*Imagine A Faith*
*A faith that is not afraid.*
*A faith that is not ashamed.*
*Imagine a faith that has real liberty.*
*Imagine a faith that makes you have real life experiences.*

For too long, fear has held us back. We’ve hesitated to explore for ourselves who Jesus truly is.  

We’ve been cautious, waiting for others to define our faith instead of stepping forward to discover the depth of a personal relationship with Him. 

But faith was never meant to be second-hand—it’s meant to be lived, breathed, and experienced first-hand.  

For generations, we’ve unknowingly surrendered our right to seek, learn, and understand. We’ve accepted a faith shaped by traditions, assumptions, and the experiences of others. 

But the gospel of Jesus Christ is not a borrowed belief—

it is a living truth that brings healing, not only to our hearts but to entire communities. It restores, renews, and strengthens.  

And now, it’s time to reclaim what was always meant to be ours.  

We are not lost. We are not defeated. We are a people called to uncover truth, to walk in revelation, and to experience the fullness of faith.  

*"God has hidden His wisdom from those who think they have it all figured out, but He has revealed it to those with open hearts."* 

*"It is God’s joy to conceal mysteries, and the joy of those who seek wisdom to uncover them."* 

The revelation of Jesus, the Chosen One, is for everyone who dares to seek it. We can know Him. We can experience Him. We can walk in the reality of His truth. 

*Imagine A Faith*
*A faith that is not afraid.*
*A faith that is not ashamed.*
*Imagine a faith that has real liberty.*
*Imagine a faith that makes you have real life experiences.*
